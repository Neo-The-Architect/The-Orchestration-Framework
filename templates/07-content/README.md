# 07-content/

## Purpose

Everything content-related: the calendar, the hook bank, content pillar definitions, the performance log, captions, derivative-content scripts. The operator's content production pipeline lives here.

## What goes here

- **Content calendar.** What is scheduled to publish, when, on which platform. Forward-looking; updated as items are scheduled.
- **Hook bank.** Reusable opening lines, framings, and structural patterns the operator can draw from when drafting new content. Append as you discover things that work.
- **Pillar definitions.** The content categories the operator commits to working within. Each pillar has a one-paragraph definition that establishes the scope and excludes what does not belong.
- **Performance log.** A running record of what was published, when, on which platform, and how it performed. Maintained by the [Autonomous Operator's Friday performance pull](../../rhythms/weekly.md).
- **Captions and derivative scripts.** Caption text for images, scripts for short-form derivatives, alt text, all the small pieces of supporting content production.
- **Long-form drafts in progress.** Drafts that have not yet been scheduled. When a draft becomes scheduled content, it moves into the calendar entry for its publish date.

## What does not go here

- **Unpublished raw ideas** (those live in `03-ideas/` until they are committed to as content).
- **Research that supports content** (that lives in `05-research/`; the content directory references the research, does not duplicate it).
- **Project-specific content production** (lives inside `08-projects/<project>/content/` if the project has its own content output that does not feed the operator's main content channels).

## Update cadence

Daily or near-daily for the calendar (as content is drafted, scheduled, published). Weekly for the performance log (the Friday pull). Monthly for the pillar definitions and hook bank (during the [system audit](../../rhythms/monthly.md), or when the operator notices a pattern that deserves to be added to the bank).

| Actor | Access |
| --- | --- |

All actors have full access. Content is one of the highest-value collaboration surfaces with the agent stack — the Strategic Counsel drafts, the operator edits, the Tactical Builder formats and prepares for publish, the specialist scheduling tool publishes. The vault is where the work coordinates.
