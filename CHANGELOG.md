## [0.2.0] - 2026-05-04

### Added
- **Philosophy: 7. The Fundamentals Discipline** (`philosophy/07-fundamentals-discipline.md`) — foundational engineering knowledge as the durable layer underneath surface trends. Names ten disciplines operators ground in (AI/ML engineering, computer intelligence, AI intelligence, business intellect, operating systems, RAG, systematic architecture, agentic AI, generative AI, enterprise cloud development).
- **Architecture: Security Layer for Foreign Code** (`architecture/security-layer-for-foreign-code.md`) — verification gate for adoption-time safety of external repos, MCPs, skills, connectors. Stages: intake, static scan, dependency provenance, permission diff, sandbox execution, verdict artifact.
- **Architecture: Knowledge Architecture as Compounding Infrastructure** (`architecture/knowledge-architecture-as-compounding-infrastructure.md`) — the vault as compounding asset; the mechanism by which operator learning compounds across engagements rather than churns.
- **Architecture: Anonymization Protocol for Public Artifacts** (`architecture/anonymization-protocol-for-public-artifacts.md`) — locked verification language and corrective procedures that keep the operator's private exclusion list out of public-facing content. Methodology gift for any operator maintaining hybrid public-plus-private workspaces.

### Changed
- **README.md**: opening thesis reframed — Orchestration Framework named explicitly as "core IP for AI-native business operations" alongside its existing positioning as a working constitution. Final clause adds the framework's compounding-asset framing.
- **README.md**: status line updated to v0.2 with summary of new content.
- **philosophy/README.md**: updated to reflect seven philosophical foundations (was six). New row added to the table.
- **architecture/README.md**: directory listing updated with three new architecture documents.
